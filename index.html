This is an Introduction to Artificial Intelligence.

We can define artificial intelligence as: The study and the carry out of techniques which need intelligence on the part of a human. We can also define it as the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines for example, computer systems.

Artificial intelligence was first created in 1950 by John McCarthy and in the same year Alan Turing started to talk about this reality on an article entitled "Computing Machinery and Intelligence".

It works through algorithms that act from programming rules and its subset Machine Learning (ML) and the different ML techniques such as Deep Learning (DL).

There are two branches of AI :

1. Machine Learning (ML).

2. Deep Learning (DL).
