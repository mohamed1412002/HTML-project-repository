Advantages of Artificial Intelligence:

1. Automates processes: As it allows robots to develop repetitive process and tasks automatically.

2. Enhance creative tasks: It saves time so it makes people free and allows them to spend more time in creative functions.

3. Provides precision: It provides greater precision than humans, for example in industrial machines it can make decisions that was made before.

4. Reduces human error.

5.Increases productivity and quality in production.
